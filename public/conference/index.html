Rising to the challenge

What it takes for African companies, societies and economies to meet the changing global risk landscape.

KIGALI - RWANDA

28 - 33 October 2021


Description

This conference will bring together representatives of risk management associations, practitioners, risk officers, researchers, regulators, and policy-makers to share their insights on the main issues that affect risk management in current African risk landscape and how to advance the practice through collaboration. The conference theme focuses on redefining the risk management agenda in Africa to ensure African organisations and governments are prepared to deal with risks that they face today.
